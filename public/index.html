About Me

Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Shiva. A Computer Science graduate student at Clark University and a backend engineer who cares about the parts of software users never see — the APIs, queues, caches, and pipelines that make everything else feel fast and reliable.

Over 3+ years across Connex AI and IdeaEdu, I've engineered Python/FastAPI services that cut API latency by 20%, optimized PostgreSQL schemas and queries for 30% faster execution, and shipped LangChain/OpenAI pipelines for document summarization and intent classification. I've built systems end-to-end: REST APIs with OAuth2/JWT auth, Redis caching and rate limiting, Kafka and RabbitMQ messaging, and CI/CD automation with GitHub Actions to AWS.

My goal is to build backend systems where performance is measured, not assumed — bringing production discipline to AI-integrated products and clean, reusable engineering to every service I ship.
